Gulper Slurry Recipe

Gulper slurry visually resembles radstag stew. Unlike most other items created at cooking stations, it does not restore health. Instead, it has the same effect as a Stealth Boy, though for only one third the duration.In Survival mode, consuming slurry restores both hunger and thirst.
